# brand.md — IqUp (North Macedonia) Brand Source-of-Truth

> Working brand reference for the IqUp marketing-campaign website (bilingual: Macedonian default / English). Factual claims are anchored to iqup.mk, iqupglobal.com, the official IqUp Macedonia Facebook page, and the confirmed partner Svetot na Bibi (svetotnabibi.mk). **All VISUAL identity items are DRAFT, pending confirmation against official brand files.**

## TL;DR
- IqUp ("IQ UP!") is the first Macedonian educational franchise for children — an after-school STEAM-plus-coding-plus-literature program built on a neuroscience-informed, story-driven methodology, operating **10 centers across North Macedonia** and targeting parents of children aged **3–13**.
- IqUp is part of the **"Svetot na Bibi" (The World of Bibi)** group; the licensed Bibi animated characters (Bibi, Bobi, Oliver and friends) are a confirmed, core brand asset woven into IqUp's curriculum, textbooks, and every lesson.
- Brand voice is warm, aspirational, parent-reassuring and child-magical ("We awaken the genius in every child"); copy is primarily Macedonian for the local audience, with a parallel English global brand.

## Key Findings
- **Identity & origin:** IqUp launched in 2022 as the first Macedonian educational franchise for children, created by the team behind the established children's brand "Svetot na Bibi" (founded 2018). The Skopje–Aerodrom center opened first, then the network expanded across the country.
- **Method:** A proprietary "much more than STEAM" model = STEAM (science, technology, engineering, arts, math) + literature + coding, grounded in experiential learning (claim: up to 95% knowledge retention through direct experience) and educational neuroscience.
- **Programs:** Five age-banded annual (9-month) programs from ages 3 to 13, each anchored by Bibi/Bobi/Oliver story characters.
- **Locations:** 10 centers — Skopje (Aerodrom, Karpoš), Veles, Štip, Ohrid, Kičevo, Kavadarci, Prilep, Gevgelija, Strumica.
- **Partnership:** Svetot na Bibi is a Macedonian animation/production company; its characters appear on IqUp's sites and in its curriculum as the narrative engine of every class.
- **Reach:** International franchise expansion (Macedonia and Chicago established; Croatia, Serbia, Albania referenced as openings); modest but engaged local social following, backed by the much larger Bibi audience.

## Details

### 1. Company overview
**What it is.** IqUp (styled "IQ UP!" with the exclamation mark as part of the wordmark) is an after-school educational franchise for children. It describes itself as "прва македонска едукативна франшиза за деца" (the first Macedonian educational franchise for children). The North Macedonia operation runs under the domain **iqup.mk** (Macedonian); the global/English brand is **iqupglobal.com**. The legal entity behind the brand is **IKUP d.o.o., Todor Aleksandrov No. 4, 1000 Skopje, North Macedonia** (per the franchise data-controller notice).

**Mission / positioning statement.** The recurring brand line is: *"Со IQ Up! ја будиме генијалноста во секое дете и го подготвуваме за предизвиците на 21 век"* ("With IqUp we awaken the genius in every child and prepare them for the challenges of the 21st century"). The global tagline is **"Education that inspires" / "Едукација која инспирира."**

**Educational philosophy.** Core belief: *"секое дете има неограничен мозочен потенцијал"* ("every child has unlimited brain potential") and that every child can learn anything but has different needs and learning styles. The concept is grounded in educational neuroscience ("неуронаука" / "neuroscientific principles") and experiential, sensory learning ("сензорно учење"), replacing rote memorization ("учење на памет") with practical understanding. A frequently cited statistic: up to **95% of knowledge gained through direct experience is retained**. The Benjamin Franklin quote — "Tell me and I forget, teach me and I may remember, involve me and I learn" — frames the method.

**History & origin.** IqUp was created by the group behind "Светот на Биби" (Svetot na Bibi / The World of Bibi), an established children's edutainment brand. Per the iqup.mk "За нас" page, IqUp was built "as part of the group of the already proven educational brand for children 'The World of Bibi.'" It was created by "parents with a strong desire and will" alongside "a team of entrepreneurs, educators, animators, engineers, and experts in mathematics, art, science and technology." The brand was publicly promoted around the **Skopje Book Fair in May 2022**, with Valentina Dinova named as a partner of the new project; the Skopje–Aerodrom center was the first to open, with the franchise network then rolling out to other cities.

**Global ↔ Macedonia relationship.** IqUp is structured as a franchise. North Macedonia is the home market and original operation; iqupglobal.com markets the franchise opportunity internationally. Per the Franchising.eu listing, "Franchises are currently established in Macedonia and Chicago, with new locations set to open in Croatia, Serbia, and Albania this September… Discussions with potential partners in Poland and Greece are also underway" (the listing also cites projected per-center revenues of €70,000–100,000). The same source states joining requires "a one-time franchise fee of 6,000 EUR," plus "furnishing and equipping the premises, estimated between 4,000 and 6,000 EUR" and teacher training "at an estimated cost of 1,000 EUR"; a separate Franchise Direct/FranchiseEurope listing notes "Master Franchise opportunities begin at €25,000." The iqup.mk blog references operations across "Macedonia, Serbia, America and other countries." Treat all franchise economics as third-party/secondary.

**Ages served.** Marketing targets **parents of children 3–13**. Note an internal inconsistency on the sites: several pages still say programs cover **5–13** ("деца на возраст од 5 до 13 години"), while the newest program ("Мали Истражувачи / Little Explorers") and current copy extend the range to **3–13**. For the campaign, **3–13 is the current, intended range.**

**What makes it distinctive.** Six stated pillars (Macedonia site "Зошто IQ UP!"): (1) unique, innovative methodology; (2) outstanding interdisciplinary programs; (3) inspirational textbooks (scientific tales + logic/brain exercises); (4) custom didactic materials; (5) modern classrooms designed as "fun laboratories"; (6) trained and certified teachers. The single biggest differentiator vs. local competitors is the **integration of STEM + coding + literature into one system, narrated by proprietary licensed cartoon characters.**

### 2. Program details
**Curriculum architecture.** Programs are age-banded and run as a **9-month annual program**. Each annual program contains (global "spectrum" figures): **40 different topics, ~300 scientific terms, 100 experiments, 100 real projects, 40 educational science stories, 40 digital coding projects, 50 brain exercises, 120 logic exercises/tasks, and 5 workbooks (4 for STEAM + 1 for coding).** (Per-age pages vary slightly, e.g., 200+ terms for younger bands, 300 for older.)

**The five programs:**
1. **Мали Истражувачи / "Little Explorers" — ages 3–5.** Gentle, playful introduction to STEM; cognitive development, scientific thinking, engineering/design play, math foundations, creativity, social-emotional and physical (motor-skill) development, plus early literacy via Bibi/Bobi stories. Framed with the Margaret Mead quote "Children must be taught how to think, not what to think."
2. **Магичната лабораторија на Биби и Боби / "Bibi and Bobby's Magic Laboratory" — ages 5–8.** Curiosity-building across 40 core topics in biology, physics, chemistry, math, engineering and technology; introduction to block-based/visual programming in the last part of each class.
3. **Магичната лабораторија на Биби и Боби ПЛУС / "Magic Laboratory PLUS" — ages 6–9.** Deeper dive; adds astronomy, electronics, botany, anatomy, geometry, chemical reactions; more advanced block coding and digital projects (kids create games/animations).
4. **Научните авантури на Оливер / "Oliver's Scientific Adventures" — ages 9–12.** Led by "10-year-old genius Oliver"; moves children from observers to independent inventors; logical reasoning, self-directed research, turning ideas into digital solutions (apps, games, digital stories).
5. **Научните авантури на Оливер ПЛУС / "Oliver's Scientific Adventures Plus" — ages 10–13.** Focus on individuality, leadership, critical thinking; students develop and build their own real + digital projects in their area of interest; framed with Einstein's "Everybody is a genius…" quote.

**What a class looks like (3-part structure).** (1) The lesson **opens with an interactive story** on a chosen topic, told by the characters Bibi, Bobi and Oliver in a simple, fun way; (2) **hands-on core** — children are directly involved: research, experimentation, analysis, real projects, interactive games, brain exercises and logic tasks, gaining functional/applied knowledge; (3) **coding finale** — children turn what they learned into digital projects (animations, games, apps) using age-appropriate programming tools.

**Classrooms.** Designed to look like **"fun laboratories"** ("забавни лаборатории") — colorful and playful for the youngest, innovative and appealing for teenagers.

**Teachers.** Experts in natural and technical sciences who are **trained and certified in the IqUp methodology**, with added psychology/pedagogy skills. Brand line: "The best teachers are those who give children directions on where to find the answer, but not the answer." Teaching is individualized; mistakes are encouraged ("ги охрабруваме грешките") and the process is celebrated over the result.

**Skills developed.** Problem-solving, creativity, critical thinking, communication, teamwork, logic, memory, analytical thinking, self-confidence, curiosity, empathy.

### 3. The Svetot na Bibi partnership
**Nature of the partnership.** Svetot na Bibi (Светот на Биби / "The World of Bibi") is the **parent/sibling brand** from which IqUp emerged — IqUp's own "About us" states IqUp was created "as part of the group of the already proven educational brand for children 'The World of Bibi.'" The Bibi characters are therefore a **confirmed, licensed, owned/in-group brand asset** available to IqUp, not a mere marketing tie-in.

**The company.** **Светот на Биби ДОО** is a Macedonian creative and production company, **founded 2018**, building a multimedia children's brand spanning animated series, educational videos, picture books, music, apps and licensed products. It builds an original IP brand with a structured IP-management system and a regional/international expansion strategy; localized in multiple languages and present on digital platforms, TV channels and retail chains across the region. Website: svetotnabibi.mk (e-commerce store). Brand-of-trust line: a brand that aims to be "a symbol of trust, quality and education."

**Creators / rights-holders.** The series was started in late 2017 (in the offices of Fakulteti.mk) by **Stojče Koko Ǵorgiev, Marjan Aleksovski and Žarko Stojanoski**. The character was drawn by **Mihajlo Dimitrievski ("The Mičo")** (original art director); voiced by **Slaǵana Vujoševiḱ** (Bibi); character/personality modeling by **Biljana Crvenkovska**; directed by **Goce Cvetanovski**. The first cartoon, "Азбуката на Биби" (Bibi's Alphabet), premiered 14 October 2018.

**The characters (cast).** Core: **Bibi** (the lead — a small, curious, likable heroine; notably wears a **yellow dress, not pink**, plays football and trains karate — deliberately breaking gender stereotypes; a "знајко"/know-it-all who shares advice, good behavior and positive energy), **Bobi** (Bobby), **Oliver** (the "10-year-old genius," a newer character created for IqUp's older programs), and the "зазулчиња." Extended universe: **Zazi, Zina** (from planet Zaz), the robot **R1 (R-1)**, and **Žiži, Dino, Alfej, Daro (dinosaur), Vera (squirrel), Fi, "Dedo Mraz od Zaz"** and many more.

**Scale of the Bibi property (credibility signals).** The "Светот на Биби" YouTube channel has **over 90,000 subscribers** (per the Ananas.mk store listing: *"Каналот на Јутјуб на Светот на Биби има повеќе од 90 илјади претплатници, што укажува на популарноста на овој бренд"*). Svetot na Bibi has cited **16 million YouTube views in 1.5 years of existence** (deca.mk, quoting the team: *"Горди сме на 16 милионите прегледи на јутјуб-каналот за година и пол постоење"*) and **≈70,000 picture books already in homes** (*"Околу 70.000 сликовници се веќе во вашите домови"*, deca.mk). It also runs the video platform video.bibi.mk (1,000+ minutes of original Macedonian animation), a 190-sticker album, a mobile app (ages 2–8), co-branded products with **Becutan, Philips, Cool Bottles, April22**, and a publishing deal with Laguna (Serbia). The feature-film project **"Златната пеперутка" (The Golden Butterfly)** won the **MIA Development Award for Outstanding Animation Project at MIA Market 2025 in Rome** (€5,000) — confirmed on MIA Market's official 2025 awards page: *"MIA DEVELOPMENT AWARD FOR OUTSTANDING ANIMATION PROJECT: The Golden Butterfly / Zlatnata Peperutka by Goce Cvetanovski, produced by Makedonska Krepost, co-produced by Svetot na Bibi, Toon Motion Studios…"* (North Macedonia, Spain, Argentina, Brazil, Colombia). A new international series "The Toothstars" is being developed via InnovaRights & Licensing (a JV of DexyCo and Svetot na Bibi).

**How/where used by IqUp.** The characters are the **narrative engine of the curriculum** — every class opens with a Bibi/Bobi/Oliver story; the programs are literally named after them ("Bibi and Bobby's Magic Laboratory," "Oliver's Scientific Adventures"); textbooks are filled with "inspirational stories from the scientific adventures of our characters." They appear visually across iqup.mk and iqupglobal.com program imagery.

### 5. Brand voice & tone
**Overall.** Warm, encouraging, aspirational and reassuring to parents; magical, playful and adventurous toward children. The brand consistently frames learning as **"magic," "adventure," and "a journey"** ("магичниот свет на науката," "авантури," "патување"). It is confident and mission-driven without being clinical.

**To parents (the buyer).** Reassurance + future-proofing. Recurring appeals: every child has unlimited potential; traditional schooling can't keep up with a fast-changing world; IqUp fills the gap; this is an "investment in your child's future." Direct address ("Драги родители," "вашето дете") and clear CTAs ("Закажете бесплатен пробен час" / "Register for a free trial class"). Trust signals: certified teachers, proven methodology, neuroscience basis, the established Bibi brand.

**To children (the user).** Playful, imaginative, character-led. Children are cast as "малите сонувачи" (little dreamers) and "мали научници, креатори и иноватори" (little scientists, creators and innovators). Energetic Facebook/event copy: "Замисли. Истражи. Создај!" ("Imagine. Explore. Create!").

**Recurring phrases / motifs (Macedonian → English):**
- "Едукација која инспирира" → "Education that inspires"
- "Ја будиме генијалноста во секое дете" → "We awaken the genius in every child"

**Quotes used.** Albert Einstein ("Everybody is a genius…"; "It's not that I'm smart…"), Benjamin Franklin ("Tell me and I forget…"), Margaret Mead ("Children must be taught how to think…"), Gordon B. Hinckley ("You can't build a great building on weak foundations").

**Language use.** Macedonian (Cyrillic) is the default and primary language for the local parent audience on iqup.mk and the Macedonia Facebook page; English is the global brand language (iqupglobal.com, LinkedIn). ### 7. Positioning & messaging
**Core value propositions:**
1. **More than STEM** — among the few programs integrating STEM + coding + literature in one system ("многу повеќе од СТЕАМ едукација").
2. **Learning as adventure** — story-led, hands-on, lab-based; children become creators, not passive listeners.
3. **Future-readiness** — builds functional knowledge + life/intellectual skills (logic, critical thinking, problem-solving, creativity) for "the 21st century / a world reshaped by AI."
4. **Every child is a genius** — individualized approach honoring different learning styles.
5. **Proven & trusted** — built on the established Bibi brand, certified teachers, neuroscience-based methodology.

**Promises to parents:** better school results, lasting/applied knowledge, confidence, love of learning, and preparation for the future.

**Differentiators / trust signals:** first Macedonian educational franchise; certified teachers; proprietary methodology and textbooks; purpose-built "laboratory" classrooms; the licensed Bibi character universe; international franchise network. A franchise-recruitment claim states that "**85% of parents who attend a single demo lesson enroll their child** because they see the transformation happening in real time" (per the IQ UP! listing on Franchise Direct/FranchiseEurope) — treat as a franchise marketing claim, not independently verified.

**Taglines/slogans:** "Education that inspires / Едукација која инспирира"; "We awaken the genius in every child"; "From atoms to algorithms" (global); "Imagine. Explore. Create." (campaign-style).

**Primary conversion action:** the **free trial / demo class** ("бесплатен пробен час / демо час"), routed to the nearest center. This should be the dominant CTA on the campaign site.

### 8. Competitive / market context
North Macedonia has a small but active children's STEM/robotics market. Notable players: **STEM Академија (STEM Academy)** (Young Engineers + LEGO Education + FIRST LEGO League partner; robotics, coding, camps), **Robotics.mk** (LEGO Education, robotics), and grassroots/competition organizers like **CTRI** (inclusive robotics). Most competitors center on **robotics + LEGO kits + coding**. IqUp's differentiation is its **broader curriculum (full STEAM + literature + coding, not robotics-kit-centric), its proprietary story-driven methodology, its own licensed character IP, and its multi-city franchise footprint** — making it more of an integrated "education concept" than a robotics club. IqUp positions itself as a category leader: "Macedonia gets a new leader in educational franchises."
