The payoff: a strengths reveal & a shareable keepsake

The /result screen and the awareness-engine certificate. One scroll moves from the playful child reveal into the calm parent handoff. Every surface extends the 1.03 tokens, reuses the strength colours + glyphs, binds only to the existing hand-off data, and shows no score, IQ, percentage, bar, gauge or ranking — anywhere. What Code builds · what’s reused · the guardrails

Modules for 1.10

  • ResultHero — reveal, child name as hero
  • StrengthsConstellation + TierGroup ×3 (celebrated / also / growing)
  • StrengthBadge (celebrated) · reuse StrengthChip (also/growing)
  • CertificatePreview + CertificateActions
  • TrialInvite (3–5 / 6–9) · CuriousMindEnding (10–13)
  • Reused: SiteHeader, SiteFooter, LanguageToggle, Button, Card, Glyph, Reveal/MotionProvider

Hard guardrails — verified

  • No score, IQ, %, points, bars, gauges or 1st/2nd/3rd medals. Internal total/hits/ratio only ever order the tiers — never shown.
  • Bibi = licensed art only. Abstract placeholder in an exact drop-in box; never generated or redrawn.
  • “Growing” reads as potential, never weakness.
  • WCAG 2.2 AA: strength colours hit contrast on their tints; focus-visible everywhere; alt text on the certificate; reduced-motion safe.
